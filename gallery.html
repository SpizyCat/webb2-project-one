This is a Porsche Panamera Turbo S, it has a twin turbocharged V8 and 620 horsepower at stock. It also has an Executive version, the main difference is the price, the Executive version also has a little bit more legroom which can be nice so it doesn't get so cramped in the car, the price difference is 177,000$ vs 188,000$ for the Executive version. I feel like this price is justified since I am a perfectionist I like to have lots of leg room for my passengers.

This is a 1972 Ford Mustang Mach 1, it is my dream car and I would love to have one of these. It uses a 302ci, 2 barrel V8 engine, with a sport interior with high bucket seats to give you that old school look. The main differenace between the 72 and the 71 version was that it actually lost the pop-open gas cap, it got replaced with the crappy standard gas twist on cap. Machs 1 are usually powered by the 302 windsor in base trim, but you could upgrade to a 351 cleveland v8 if you really wanted to maximize the power out of this monster.
